Project Overview
Abstact
BigRecipe is a website which gives information about different recipes to the users. Users can search the recipe, can see the most searched recipes on BigOven Recipe using popular tab. Users can look at different cuisines and see recipes of different varieties.

Recipe details includes recipe title, recipe description, preparation time, ratings, cuisine, category, ingredients details and recipe method. If available, users can also watch Youtube Videos of similar recipes.
Functionalities
Home
It displays different varieties of recipe. On click of a image it will show images and titles of recipe's in that particular variety. Now, on click of this recipe's images it will show recipe details of that particular item.
Cuisine
Initially it will display italian cuisine recipes. User can select mexican, indian or american cuisines and accordingly images of its recipes will appear. Now, on click of a button 'View Recipe' recipe details of the recipe whose image is currenly being watched will appear in a Tab menu.
Search
It allows user to search a recipe by specifying its name in search box. It will return recipe's images and titles. I am displaying four recipes at a time. On click of a button 'Recipe' it will display recipe details in a Tab Menu.
Popular
It will show four item's images and titles whose recipes been seen most times by searching in a search tab. On click of a image it will show recipe details in a tab menu.
